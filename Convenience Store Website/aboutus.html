Our Store

Our most popular, burgers and chicken. It’s a decidedly simple concept on the surface. But try our foods and you are struck by the taste of our food perfection. Since opening in Fall 1999, DBF store has developed a devoted following of food lovers, DBF store strategically positioned throughout for hungry diners at all hours of the day and night. Since it’s impossible to be at the right place at all times, DBF store opened in November of 1999 to keep up with increasing demand. Simply put, DBF store is obsessed with creating the perfect burger. With 30 years of fine dining restaurant and catering experience, owner Zhi Chang believes even “regular” food becomes extraordinary when executed at a high level. DBf store’s chef-driven approach to a mass appeal food is a key to DBF store’s success. Zhi Chang boasts a long career in fine dining, having worked front of house in Enoteca Vin, Pop’s, Nana’s, and Il Palio in the Triangle as well as Wente Brothers Sparkling Wine Cellar & Restaurant in Livermore, CA. This experience in food and a dedication to using only the finest ingredients can be tasted in every bite at DBF store. Burgers and chickens are made fresh to order. No corners are cut to deliver the finest burger and chickento DBF store diners. With recent stories in the New York Times, Durham Magazine, Martha Stewart Living and the Raleigh News & Observer and its recent runner-up finish in the national America’s Favorite Food Truck Giveaway on The Food Network, DBF store is poised for greater things in the years to come. DBF store…when only the best will do!
